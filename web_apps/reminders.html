How typing works

Type plain English in the title or notes and the app recognizes dates, times, and repeats. When it spots something it shows an Auto-apply settings for… prompt — press Enter (title) or Ctrl+Enter (notes), or click Apply. The schedule fields below accept the same phrases and show a live Detected badge.

How it works

Type a natural-language phrase or G-code on any line, then press Enter to convert it. If your cursor is at the end of the line a new line is created; if mid-line the text is converted in place. Lines that are already valid G-code are left unchanged.

Quick Setup

quick setup mill T1 H1→T01 M06; G00 G90 … M08;
quick setup mill T2 H2 3000 RPM→T02 M06; … S3000 M03; …
qsm T3 H3 4500→short alias, RPM optional

Motion

arc clockwise to X1 Y1 R1→G02 X1 Y1 R1
